Before You Go

Essential Travel Tips

Make the most of your Sri Lanka journey with these helpful pointers from seasoned travellers.

01
Best Time to Visit
December to March is ideal for the south and west coasts. April to September is perfect for the east coast and hill country. Sri Lanka has something to offer year-round!
02
Visa & Entry
Most nationalities require an ETA (Electronic Travel Authorization) obtainable online before arrival. Apply at eta.gov.lk — it's quick and easy.
03
Getting Around
Scenic train journeys are a must — especially Kandy to Ella. Tuk-tuks are perfect for short distances. PickMe app works like Uber and is highly recommended.
04
Currency & Costs
Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). ATMs are widely available in cities. Sri Lanka is budget-friendly — a local meal can cost as little as 200–400 LKR.
05
Cultural Respect
Dress modestly when visiting temples — cover shoulders and knees. Remove shoes before entering sacred spaces. Always ask before photographing locals.
06
Food & Drink
Try kottu roti, hoppers, and fresh coconut. Street food is delicious and affordable. Drink bottled water. Sri Lankan spices are bold — tell your host if you prefer mild!
